Formicarium

How to keep an ant colony at home

FORMICARIUM. A formicarium, also known as an ant farm, is a special type of vivarium designed for the study of ant colonies and their behavior. It is typically made of a clear, flat surface, such as glass or acrylic, with a layer of soil or sand for the ants to build their nest in. Formicaria can be used to observe and research the social behaviors and interactions of different ant species, as well as the formation and maintenance of their nests and colonies. Myrmecologists, scientists who study ants, often use formicaria in their research. The formicarium was invented by Charles Janet, a French entomologist, and has been used for educational and research purposes since its introduction in the early 20th century.
